Career Outline





I am a budding IT specialist with a background in business and architecture. I believe my past knowledge and experience will bring a fresh perspective to the IT industry.

I am studying a Higher Diploma in Computing in the Dublin Institute of Technology (DIT) to fully develop my knowledge of computer science. I already have had extensive experience of working with computers, most notably Photoshop, CAD, sketch-up, In-design and other design orientated software.

In 2004 I enrolled on a degree course in Architectural Technology in DIT, graduating in 2007 with a 2.1 Award. I began working in the National Building Agency in summer 2007 and I left that company to continue my Architectural education in 2009. In 2009 I enrolled in the University College Dublin (UCD) Architecture programme. Graduating in 2012 with a 2.1 Degree award, I became a PA/Architectural Adviser to a restaurateur during the day, enrolling in a Certificate in Business Operations in Dublin Business School in the evening.

In March 2013 I began my career in ICT. Computer Science, especially software design and programming, is a very interesting field to be involved in. My subjects include Object-Orientated software Design, Web and User Interface Design, Systems Analysis and Testing and Information Systems. I will choose to specialise in Software Development or Data Analytics towards the end of the course.
